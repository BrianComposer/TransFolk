TransFolk

Transformer-based Symbolic Folk Melody Generator

Generate original folk melodies with a transformer-based model. Select your model, start from a MusicXML prompt or guide the output by meter and tonality, and refine results using advanced sampling controls such as temperature, top-k, top-p, and repetition penalty. Instantly play back the generated score and download it in MusicXML or MIDI format.
